## How to sign

Signing is automated by the **CLA Assistant** workflow
([`.github/workflows/cla.yml`](.github/workflows/cla.yml)). When you open a pull
request, the `CLA Assistant` status check goes red and the bot comments asking
you to sign. Sign **once** (covering all future PRs) by posting this exact
comment on the PR:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your signature is recorded in `signatures/version1/cla.json` on the
`cla-signatures` branch; the check then turns green. PRs cannot be merged until
it passes — this protects every user's ability to rely on the project's
licensing. Bots are allow-listed. See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md).
